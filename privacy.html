Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

At HiredAI Labs, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our products and services, including the Rewind photo cleanup app and other HiredAI Labs products.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using our products and services, you consent to our collection and use of personal information as outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Rewind App - Photos and Videos

Local Processing Only: Rewind does not upload your photos or videos to our servers. All photo processing, analysis, and sorting happens entirely on your device.

  • We never access, store, or transmit your photo files
  • We never access metadata from your photos
  • All image analysis happens locally on your device
  • Session history and sorting preferences are stored locally on your device

2.2 Analytics and Usage Data

We may collect anonymous analytics data to understand how our products are being used and to improve them. This may include:

  • App usage statistics (features used, session duration)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • General geographic location (country/region level only)

This data is collected through analytics services like Google Analytics and does not include personal identifying information or your photo library.

2.3 Contact Information

If you contact us through our website contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message content

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Product Improvement: We use analytics data to improve our products and services
  • Customer Support: To respond to your support requests and feedback
  • Technical Operations: To maintain and troubleshoot our services
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud or security issues
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations

4. Photo Privacy Guarantees

For Rewind App specifically:

  • Your photos and videos never leave your device
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your image files
  • We do not have access to your photo library on our servers
  • We do not sell or share your photos with third parties
  • If you delete photos from Recently Deleted, they are permanently deleted from your device
  • All sorting and deletion decisions are made entirely on your device

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where Data is Stored

For services that do transmit data (contact forms, analytics):

  • Analytics data is stored by Google Analytics on secure, encrypted servers
  • Contact form submissions are sent via encrypted email
  • Contact information is stored securely and not shared with third parties

5.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Third-Party Services

Our products may use third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage analytics
  • EmailJS: For secure email delivery of contact form submissions
  • App Stores: App Store (Apple) and Play Store (Google) may collect usage data

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

  • For Rewind: All your data is stored locally on your device. You have complete control and can delete it at any time
  • For contact information: You can request to see, update, or delete information you've provided to us

7.2 Analytics Opt-Out

You can opt-out of analytics collection by disabling data collection in your device settings or through your app settings.

7.3 Device Permissions

You control which permissions you grant to our apps. You can modify app permissions at any time through your device settings.

8. Data Retention

  • Rewind App: All data is stored on your device until you delete it
  • Contact Form Submissions: Kept until resolved, then securely deleted
  • Analytics Data: Retained according to Google Analytics retention policies (typically 14-38 months)

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

If you are accessing our services from outside your country of residence, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from your home country.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, if required, by obtaining your consent. Your continued use of our services following the posting of a revised Privacy Policy means you accept the changes.

13. Additional Information by Product

13.1 Rewind - Photo Cleanup App

Rewind is designed with privacy as the core principle. Here's what you need to know:

  • Zero-Knowledge Architecture: We don't have the ability to see your photos, even if we wanted to
  • Local Processing: All AI and machine learning analysis happens on your device
  • No Cloud Syncing: Your session history and preferences are stored only on your device
  • Permissions: Rewind only requests photo library access—it doesn't request internet access for photo transmission
  • Deletion: When you permanently delete photos, they are securely removed from your device

14. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, the legal basis for processing your information is:

  • Consent: You have given us clear consent to process your information
  • Contract: Processing is necessary to provide our services
  • Legal Obligation: Processing is required by law
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests (e.g., improving services)

15. Rights Under GDPR and Similar Laws

If you are in the EU, UK, Canada, or another jurisdiction with similar privacy laws, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Request data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@hiredailabs.com.
