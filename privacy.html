Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

At HiredAI Labs, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our products and services, including:

  • ReceptorAI: AI receptionist for dental practices
  • Rewind: Photo cleanup app

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using our products and services, you consent to our collection and use of personal information as outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Rewind App - Photos and Videos

Local Processing Only: Rewind does not upload your photos or videos to our servers. All photo processing, analysis, and sorting happens entirely on your device.

  • We never access, store, or transmit your photo files
  • We never access metadata from your photos
  • All image analysis happens locally on your device
  • Session history and sorting preferences are stored locally on your device

2.2 Rewind - Optional Google Sign In

Sign-in is completely optional. Rewind works perfectly without signing in. Here's what happens if you choose to sign in:

  • We create an account linked to your Google account
  • Your session history and preferences are stored securely
  • Your photos are never synced, uploaded, or stored on our servers
  • You can sign out anytime and continue using Rewind without signing in
  • We use anonymous IDs for users who don't sign in

2.3 Rewind - Anonymous Usage

If you use Rewind without signing in:

  • You're assigned an anonymous device ID (stored locally on your device)
  • All your session history and preferences stay on your device
  • We don't collect any personally identifiable information
  • Your usage is completely private and local

2.4 ReceptorAI - Patient Information

For dental practices using ReceptorAI, we collect only information necessary to manage patient appointments:

  • Patient names
  • Patient phone numbers
  • Appointment details and times
  • Service type being booked

We do NOT access clinical records, treatment notes, billing information, or sensitive health data from your practice management system.

2.5 Analytics and Usage Data

We may collect anonymous analytics data to understand how our products are being used and to improve them. This may include:

  • App usage statistics (features used, session duration)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • General geographic location (country/region level only)

This data is collected through analytics services like Google Analytics and does not include personal identifying information or your photo library.

2.6 Contact Information

If you contact us through our website contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message content

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Product Improvement: We use analytics data to improve our products and services
  • Customer Support: To respond to your support requests and feedback
  • Technical Operations: To maintain and troubleshoot our services
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud or security issues
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations

4. Photo Privacy Guarantees (Rewind)

Rewind is built with privacy as the core principle:

  • Your photos and videos never leave your device
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your image files
  • We do not have access to your photo library on our servers
  • We do not sell or share your photos with third parties
  • If you delete photos from Recently Deleted, they are permanently deleted from your device
  • All sorting and deletion decisions happen entirely on your device
  • Even if you sign in with Google, your photos never sync to cloud storage

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where Data is Stored

For services that do transmit data (contact forms, analytics, ReceptorAI appointments):

  • Analytics data is stored by Google Analytics on secure, encrypted servers
  • Contact form submissions are sent via encrypted email
  • ReceptorAI patient data is stored in Airtable with AES-256 encryption
  • Rewind session data (when signed in) is stored securely in cloud database
  • Rewind photos are never stored on our servers

5.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Third-Party Services

Our products may use third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage analytics
  • Google Sign In (Rewind): For optional account authentication
  • Airtable (ReceptorAI): For secure appointment data storage
  • EmailJS: For secure email delivery of contact form submissions
  • App Stores: App Store (Apple) and Play Store (Google) may collect usage data

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

  • Rewind: All your data is stored locally on your device. You have complete control and can delete it at any time
  • Rewind (Signed In): You can request to see, update, or delete your account data
  • ReceptorAI: Practice owners can request patient data access or deletion
  • Contact Information: You can request to see, update, or delete information you've provided to us

7.2 Analytics Opt-Out

You can opt-out of analytics collection by disabling data collection in your device settings or through your app settings.

7.3 Device Permissions

You control which permissions you grant to our apps. You can modify app permissions at any time through your device settings.

7.4 Rewind Sign-Out

You can sign out of your Google account at any time in Rewind settings. Your app will continue to work with an anonymous ID.

8. Data Retention

  • Rewind App: All data is stored on your device until you delete it
  • Rewind (Signed In): Kept as long as your account is active. Deleted upon account termination
  • ReceptorAI: Retained as long as practice subscription is active
  • Contact Form Submissions: Kept until resolved, then securely deleted
  • Analytics Data: Retained according to Google Analytics retention policies (typically 14-38 months)

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

If you are accessing our services from outside your country of residence, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from your home country.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our services following the posting of a revised Privacy Policy means you accept the changes.

13. Product-Specific Information

13.1 Rewind - Privacy First Architecture

Rewind is designed with privacy as the core principle. Here's the technical approach:

  • Zero-Knowledge Architecture: We don't have the ability to see your photos, even if we wanted to
  • Local Processing: All AI and machine learning analysis happens on your device
  • No Cloud Syncing: Your session history is stored only on your device (unless you sign in, then only metadata syncs)
  • Permissions: Rewind only requests photo library access—it doesn't request internet access for photo transmission
  • Deletion: When you permanently delete photos, they are securely removed from your device

13.2 Rewind - Optional Sign-In Benefits

If you choose to sign in with Google:

  • Your session history syncs across devices
  • Your sorting preferences are saved to the cloud
  • Your photos NEVER sync or upload to cloud
  • You maintain complete privacy and control

13.3 ReceptorAI - PIPEDA Compliance

ReceptorAI complies with PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act), Canada's federal privacy law:

  • Only collects patient information necessary for appointment booking
  • Does not access clinical records or sensitive health data
  • Implements AES-256 encryption for all patient data
  • Practices are the data custodians—we're data processors
  • Happy to sign Data Processing Agreements (DPAs)

14. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, the legal basis for processing your information is:

  • Consent: You have given us clear consent to process your information
  • Contract: Processing is necessary to provide our services
  • Legal Obligation: Processing is required by law
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests

15. Rights Under GDPR, PIPEDA, and Similar Laws

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Request data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@hiredailabs.com.
