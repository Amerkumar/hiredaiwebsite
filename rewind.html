Sign In (Optional)

Rewind works perfectly without signing in. We give you an anonymous ID so your session history and preferences are saved locally.

If you'd like to sync your data across multiple devices, you can optionally sign in with Google. This is completely optional—your photos are never shared regardless.

What Happens When You Sign In?

  • ✓ Your session history syncs across your devices
  • ✓ Your sorting preferences are saved to your account
  • ✓ Your photos remain on your device—they never sync to cloud
  • ✓ We don't collect any photo metadata
  • ✓ You can sign out anytime and continue using the app
