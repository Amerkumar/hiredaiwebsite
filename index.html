Implementation is simple and fast. After signing up, our team will schedule a 30-minute onboarding call to understand your practice needs. HiredAI seamlessly integrates with OpenDental and other leading practice management systems. Our integration automatically syncs appointments, patient information, and scheduling data in real-time. If you use a different system, contact us to discuss compatibility options.

Patient privacy and data security are our top priorities. HiredAI is fully compliant with Canadian privacy laws (PIPEDA) and healthcare regulations. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, stored on Canadian servers, and we maintain strict access controls. We undergo regular security audits and never share patient information with third parties.
