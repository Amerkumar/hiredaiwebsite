Why I Build

I'm a solo developer in Burnaby, BC. I build software because I like solving problems and making things that people actually use.

Every product here started with a real frustration. HiredLine came from watching tradespeople miss calls while on a job site. Rewind came from having 12,000 photos I'd never look at again.

I ship small, focused products. I iterate based on real usage. I don't take VC money or build features for hypothetical customers. If something doesn't work, I fix it or kill it.

That's it. No manifesto, no mission statement. Just craft software that does what it says.
